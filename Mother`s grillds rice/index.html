Our History

Grilled Rice is a popular variation of rice in Indonesia. The name “Grilled Rice” refers to the cooking method, which is by grilling rice that has been given spices and side dishes, usually using banana leaves as a wrapper.

Grilled Rice has various variations depending on the side dishes and spices used. Some popular variations include Nasi Bakar Chicken, Nasi Bakar Fish, and Nasi Bakar Squid. These side dishes are usually mixed with rice and spices before being wrapped and grilled, giving a rich taste and delicious aroma.

Grilled Rice is often served with chili sauce and pickles to add flavor. In addition, the savory and slightly crispy texture of the rice from the grilling process makes Grilled Rice a food that many people like.
